I am a research-oriented master student, working with Prof. Jian Peng at Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign (UIUC).

Before coming to UIUC, I worked on medical image analysis using computer vision methods at Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago (TTIC), a philanthropically endowed academic computer science institute.

Previously, I developed recommendation algorithms with deep learning in Department of Search and Big Data at JD.com (NYSE: JD).

I received B.S. degree in Statistics from University of Science and Technology of China(USTC) in 2016, working closely with Prof. Yaning Yang.
